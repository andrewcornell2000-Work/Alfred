# Market Research: AI Productised Service for Local Businesses

Deep market research to identify a simple, commercially viable AI-powered product/service for small local businesses, sellable by a solo developer at ~$999 setup + monthly fee, with **no integrations**, **direct owner purchase**, and — critically — **inbound/low-touch sales** (founder is sales-averse).

## How to read this

Start with **[09-top-5-ideas-and-recommendation.md](09-top-5-ideas-and-recommendation.md)** — it contains the scoring matrix, the ranked top-5 ideas, the final recommendation, and the sales-channel plan. Everything else is the underlying evidence.

## Method

Six parallel deep-research agents (130+ distinct web searches total) covering pain-point mining, competitive intelligence, seed-idea validation, business-model case studies, vertical analysis, and distribution channels — followed by two focused GO/NO-GO validation dives on the top-ranked concepts. All claims cite source URLs. Vendor-published numbers are flagged where used.
